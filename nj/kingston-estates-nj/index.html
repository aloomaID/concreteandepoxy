Kingston Estates, NJ Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Kingston Estates is an unincorporated community and census-designated place (CDP) located within Cherry Hill, in Camden County, in the U.S. state of New Jersey, that had been part of the Barclay-Kingston CDP until the 2000 census, which was split to form the CDPs of Barclay and Kingston Estates as of the 2010 Census.
